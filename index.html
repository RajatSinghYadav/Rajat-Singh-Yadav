Biography

I am an applied economist specializing in microeconometric methods, randomized evaluations, and data migration diagnostics. My research focuses primarily on digital financial inclusion structural design, climate resilience across marginal populations, and macroeconomic poverty index configurations.

Currently, I serve as a Research Consultant at Women's World Banking, where I structure multi-country quantitative profiles targeting institutional UX access and MSME capacity tracking. I have previously driven strategic evaluation briefs for the Asian Development Bank Institute (ADBI), The World Bank, and the Finance Department (Government of Jharkhand).
